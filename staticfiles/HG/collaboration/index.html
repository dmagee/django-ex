Collaboration

Academic Collaboration

HeteroGenius was formed by researchers from the University of Leeds (UK) and consequently has a wide range of expertise in formulating research projects and applying for UK and European academic funding (EU, EPSRC, etc.). We are happy to discuss involvement with your project at the conception stage and be involved either as a project partner or supporter. Additionally, we are happy to discuss commercial projects with universities who have either developed or conceptualised solutions to problems involving Digital Pathology, or other medical imagery. Contact us with your collaboration proposals, or for more information.

OEM Software

We can re-brand, customise and extend any of our products to the requirements of OEM software suppliers such as scanner manufacturers, Laboratory Information Systems vendors, etc. Contact us and one of our team would be happy to discuss your requirements.
